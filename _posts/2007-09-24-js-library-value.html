I thought "JavaScript Libraries in the World of AJAX" would be more of an overview of different libraries but it was conducted at a higher level of abstraction.

One of the points was a Joseph Smarr Redux: "blank pages are slow."

It didn't strike me the first time I heard him say it but this time I stopped to reflect on each page's size as it fits into a workflow. Easy to contemplate in afterthought, difficult to address in implementation as a design constraint (while also creating dynamic user experiences.)
