Yesterday's strategy was a bit old skool.

In summary, I walked up and down streets in Russian Hill looking for "For Rent" signs. I walked up on one street, went over a block, came down the parallel street, went over another block, and repeated.

I canvassed about seven streets in total at about four blocks long each. I got a couple of numbers that way, even for the apartment that I think I'll sign the lease on.
