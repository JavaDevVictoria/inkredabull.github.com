So, I heard an interview with Missy Elliott recently and I thought, "Hmm....  She sounds interesting.  I've only heard 'Pass That Dutch' and it's a catchy tune but I can't say that I know any other songs by her." What did I do?  I turned to iTunes.  I found the 'This is Not a Test' album and decided I would give it a try, using an American bank for payment.  To make a long story short, you can't do that in iTunes.  If you live in Austria, you have to have a financial instituation in Austria for the payment.  It's not even about the billing address.  Sorry Missy, I can't buy your music. —Nov 03, 2006
