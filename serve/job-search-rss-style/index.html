When I started my latest job search, I selected about 20 top-tier companies I was interested in pursuing. Oh how much simpler my life would have been if I could have just subscribed to some sort of "Software Developer" job feed at each company! Here's a suggestion to all you HR units out there - make all of your job descriptions into RSS-feeds! I tried creating my my own Google Co-Op search engine but that wound up bombing because - I suspect - of the way that the jobs exist in the Deep Web. If one of the big online job boards came up with an *easy* way to track the positions I'm in at the companies I'm interested in, that'd be great but until then, I say markup the job descriptions as feeds!!! UPDATE: I found Indeed. Awesome. You can search by job title, company, and/or geography, just to name a few of the (most important IMHO) criteria. Salary search is in there too. —Mar 13, 2007
