Just found out that the Inter-Agency Games - the UN's mini-olympics - will be held in Tyrol, Austria this year. I had the privilege of participating in Crete two years ago on the IAEA basketball team and was lucky enough to bring home two medals: the bronze and the silver! I was "borrowed" to the Geneva/Paris/Rome team (consisting of staff members from WHO/FAO/etc.) which wasn't quite large enough to compete but we came in third, hence the bronze. The IAEA team came in second, hence the silver. —Dec 11, 2006
