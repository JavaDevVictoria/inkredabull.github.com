There are Christmas trees everywhere here in the city now. When I saw them yesterday, I had to think "isn't it too soon to be buying a tree?"

Then, I remembered "it's Dec 4th! Only three more weeks until Christmas!" That got me to thinking: except for one Christmas in my life, I've always been someplace cold in December. The one time I wasn't, we (my family) were on vacation.

The novelty and anticipation of traveling and the fact that when we left Michigan and also arrived back, it was cold, lent a feeling of "it's a holiday." Here, it's so easy to forget that it's "winter."

—Dec 05, 2007
